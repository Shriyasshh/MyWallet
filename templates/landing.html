Account Management

Transaction Tracking
Record every income, expense, and transfer. Filter, search, and export your transaction history with powerful tools.
Debt Manager
Track money you've borrowed or lent. Set due dates, reminders, and mark partial payments — never lose track of a debt again.

About My Wallet

Built to simplify your financial life

My Wallet was created by a passionate developer who wanted a simple, beautiful, and secure way to manage personal finances without the complexity of traditional banking apps.

Whether you're tracking daily expenses, managing debts, or planning savings goals — My Wallet gives you the clarity and tools to take control of your money.

🐍 Django Backend 🎨 Bootstrap Frontend 🗄️ PostgreSQL 🔒 Secure Auth 📱 Responsive 🌐 Open Source
